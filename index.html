Airbnb is part of the lodging industry and takes advantage of the "sharing economy". It operates by renting out homes in a similar manner to a hotel. While it maintains the same business model as a hotel/motel, it is a more comfortable experience since it feels like you're right at home. It is especially beneficial for tourists or people with wanderlust who are often far from home. Additionally, it allows for an opportunity to explore the town you may want to move to since you're immersed right into the neighbourhood without making a permanent or long-term commitmment. It is also beneficial for those who want to rent out their homes or part of their homes. This is an easy way to make money without needing to leave your home!
